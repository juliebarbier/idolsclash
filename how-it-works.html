How to buy and support the game

We have decided to bring our product to life through a Kickstarter campaign. By launching on this platform, we aim to gather the necessary support and funding from the community. If we reach our goal of 10,000 euros, the project will transition from a mere concept to a tangible product

STEP 01

Go to the Kickstarter page of the project and Click on "Notify me on the launch"

To stay informed about the launch of our Kickstarter campaign, it's important to pre-register on Kickstarter. By doing so, you'll receive notifications and updates as soon as our campaign goes live.

Support on Kickstarter

STEP 02

Pledge the game when the campaign is open

Once logged in, navigate to the campaign page you want to support. Click the "Back this project" button, which will take you to a page where you can choose your pledge amount and select a reward tie.

Here, you can select between the Standard, Deluxe, or Art Collector editions of the game. Choose your favourite and esclusive game!

STEP 03

Payment & crowdfunding

Enter your payment information and review your pledge details.
Finally, confirm your pledge.
Your card will only be charged if the project reaches its funding goal.
By making a pledge, you're supporting the creator's vision and helping bring their project to life.
